• Very little to be gained from converting traditional forms of fiction to the online medium. Much better to just read the printed form

  • One possibility for online fiction – “shared universe”

  • Basic idea is that several authors write stories set in the same fictional universe with the same general background and many of the same characters, and let readers pursue the type of plot and character each of them find interesting

  • Most famous interactive fiction afternoon, a story - Michael Joyce (1987)
